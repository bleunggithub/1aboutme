Betty Leung    Web Developer


About me...


Hello, this is Betty. I was born and raised in Hong Kong, and I went to the UK to study since year 9. After I finished my A-levels in the UK, I took a gap year and returned to Hong Kong to work briefly. I went back to the UK afterwards to study History of Art at the University of Warwick. I returned to Hong Kong for good after my graduation and worked in several fields including company secretarial and trust.

Why am I here...


As an arts major, I have always been told that I lacked "solid skills" comparing to students graduated in business or science majors. I took different routes in my career path to find out what I want to achieve. I realise that I particularly enjoy roles that allow me to use my creativity and skills to solve problems in a people-oriented perspective. I believe that learning to code opens doors to opportunities, and I would like to make a difference in the world, however small it may be.

What I like to do in my free time...

Travel

I love travelling and experiencing different cultures and a change of scenary. It almost feels like I am living someone else's life for a few days.
Did I mention that I worked as a flight attendant briefly during my gap year too?

Cook

Cooking and baking are my passion I cannot live without! Food and science is also quite a fascinating subject.
I like to cook and bake for my friends and families, and luckily they are enjoying it too.

Hike

I usually go hiking when I travel overseas, as I feel that HK is often a little too hot and humid to hike. This allows me to see some different landscapes and reminded me to appreciate the greatness and beauty of mother nature.
